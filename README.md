# SmartBiz AI

SmartBiz AI is an AI-powered invoice and business management system designed for small businesses, freelancers, and local service providers. It allows users to generate professional invoices using natural language, manage customers and products, track invoice history, view analytics, and get smart business insights using AI.

## Project Title

SmartBiz AI: AI-Powered Invoice and Business Management System

## Project Overview

Many small businesses still create invoices manually using paper, spreadsheets, or basic templates. This process is time-consuming and can lead to calculation mistakes, poor record management, and lack of business insights.

SmartBiz AI solves this problem by allowing users to create invoices using simple natural language input. The system uses Google Gemini API to understand the transaction details and convert them into a structured invoice format.

Apart from invoice generation, the project also includes customer management, product tracking, analytics, AI business assistant, company settings, and PDF invoice download.

## Features

- AI invoice generation from natural language
- Professional invoice preview
- PDF invoice download
- Invoice history management
- Customer management
- Product and inventory tracking
- Analytics dashboard
- AI business assistant
- Company settings
- Modern responsive dashboard UI
